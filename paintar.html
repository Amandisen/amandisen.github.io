PaintAR

PaintAR is an Augmented reality android application that enables the users to draw in 3D. The project was done together with Solbritt Gateman, Johanna Lundqvist, and Haris Vidimlic during a course in Multimodal Interaction and Interfaces. The purpose of PaintAR was to study whether multimodal feedback in the form of sound and haptics could further enhance the users engagement when drawing in an AR android application.

The tool was developed using Unity with image tracking.
