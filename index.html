AuraExplora

AuraExplora is a tool that can aid people in exploring and planning their Aurora Borealis adventures by providing a map visualization of related data such as KP-index, weather forecast, and daylight time. It was created together with Adam Cerven, Haris Vidimlic, Solbritt Gateman, and Wenjia Chen during the fall of 2021 for a course in Information Visualization.

PaintAR

PaintAR is an Augmented Reality android application that enables the users to draw in 3D. The project was done together with Solbritt Gateman, Johanna Lundqvist, and Haris Vidimlic during a course in Multimodal Interaction and Interfaces. The purpose of PaintAR was to study whether multimodal feedback in the form of sound and haptics could further enhance the users immersion when drawing in an AR android application.
