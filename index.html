University Projects

AuraExplora

AuraExplora is a tool that can aid people in exploring and planning their Aurora Borealis adventures by providing a map visualization of related data such as KP-index, weather forecast, and daylight time. It was created together with Adam Cerven, Haris Vidimlic, Solbritt Gateman, and Wenjia Chen for a course in Information Visualization.

  • Learn more

PaintAR

PaintAR is an augmented reality android application that enables the users to draw in 3D. The project was done together with Solbritt Gateman, Johanna Lundqvist, and Haris Vidimlic during a course in Multimodal Interaction and Interfaces. The purpose of PaintAR was to study whether audio and haptic feedback could have a positive effect on the user's immersion and experience when painting in 3D on an AR application.

Sonification of a radiator for sustainable energy consumption

In this project we explored whether sonification of home appliances, and more particularly a radiator, can generate a more sustainable energy consumption. The project was made together with Haris Vidilmic, Solbritt Gateman, and Jiangyue Han for a course in Human Perception and Information Technology.

Read&See

Read&See allows users to find book to movie adaptations, create watchlists, and engage users to further develop the site by adding their own adaptations. It was created during the course Interaction Programming and Dynamic Webb together with Malin Svenberg, Emelie Eriksson, and Hilda Robertsson. I was in charge of data handling and the component that allows users to add their own book-movie adaptations to the database, among other things.
Duration: March - May, 2020
Technologies: JavaScript, HTML, CSS

Visualization of learning progression: A way to enhance students´ awareness and contribute to study success

Bachelor thesis written together with Philip Axelsson. The purpose was to examine how a learning dashboard integrated in a learning management system should be designed to promote students’ success in learning and enlighten them about their learning progression. Based on previous related studies, we created a low fidelity prototype of a learning dashboard using Figma. It was evaluated using think aloud tests and semi structured interviews.
Duration: January - June, 2021

About me
